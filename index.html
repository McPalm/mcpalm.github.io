Box prototyping is a lie. a god damned lie. Now the idea thing that is tries to achieve is true. Focus on what makes the game good, make only that, make the core gameplay loop work. But you just cant do that in a lot o game. Good luck making a fighting game with just boxes, you wont! Because character animations is what drives those. Case in point, a lot of times in testing, I hear things such as. Oh, this feels sluggish, I wish I could turn around faster. And the fix was increase the speed of the turn around animation. Not actually making the game physics any different, only making the feedback from the animation faster. My break through was absolutely when I decided to scrap a lot of that dev wisdom, and make an animation first platforming game.
